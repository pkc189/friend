Who Are We ?

Friends For Patna was founded in the year 2014 with a mission to boost Social Entrepreneurship by Providing Top quality resources and support to People of Patna & Bihar. The organization aims to create more social entrepreneurs and leaders who can contribute to society. In the last few years, this organization has worked passionately to boost Social Entrepreneurship and created 1000+ young leaders and change makers. In the upcoming one year it aims to reach 1 lacs in number with a vision to be a revolution in Bihar. We are always looking for more good leaders and entrepreneurs who can join us on this platform and help us in making Patna and Bihar developed.

Aditya Shankar

Founder of Friends For Patna,SC Advocate, KB Partner, BJPYM (India) Law Co-Incharge & Social Worker

Aditya Shankar Founder of Friends For Patna,SC Advocate, KB Partner, BJPYM (India) Law Co-Incharge & Social Worker Aditya Shankar is serving as Lawyer at Supreme Court and Patna High Court for Decades. He is also Co- conveyor of BJP Youth wing Legal Cell. In his leadership career, he noticed several problems that are faced by youths of Patna and Bihar. So he came with the idea of Friends for Patna which is a non for profit organization formed in 2014.
